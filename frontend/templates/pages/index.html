Taster session bookings now open

20th July 2016

Looking forward to coming back to Kent after summer? Or maybe you're just starting and want to get involved.

Either way, now is the perfect time to book your taster session to try out Sailing and Windsurfing at the University of Kent. Booking sessions ensures we have your kit ready when you get here, and saves you waiting around! Simply give us your name and email, or log in with your Kent Sports account and we'll save you a spot on the day.

If you're already a member and wouldn't mind helping us out by volunteering on safety and beach crew, just hit the link at the bottom of this page. See you on the water!

Team Racing trials date coming soon!

22nd July 2016

As well as casual weekend sailing, why not try your hand at University Team Racing?

The University of Kent has competed nationally for several years, and is growing rapidly. If you're an experienced racer, or would just like to try out something new, please watch our Facebook and Twitter for announcements of the team tryouts. Typically we train every Wednesday afternoon and hold a short theory session over drinks on Friday afternoons. It's a great chance to try out a new style of racing, or hone your skills against other universities.

If you have any questions, or want to know more please get in touch with our Race Captain!
